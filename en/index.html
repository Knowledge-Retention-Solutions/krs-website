Applied Cognitive Task Analysis (ACTA) – the gold standard for tacit knowledge. Captures the "how" and "why" behind expert decisions.

AI Power

Our AI assistant listens, structures in real-time, and prompts the questions that would otherwise be forgotten. Secure and GDPR-compliant.

Results

Expert knowledge that stays: As onboarding material for your team, as a data foundation for your AI.
