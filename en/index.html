01

The Method

Applied Cognitive Task Analysis (ACTA), the gold standard for tacit knowledge. Captures the „how" and „why" behind expert decisions.

Proven at

  • NASA: Mission-Critical Knowledge Capture
  • US Military: training of pilots and military technicians
  • Medicine: surgical training programs

Historically, the effort for preparation, transcription, and post-processing was very high. ACTA therefore only became established for high-stakes applications.

At KRS, we've evolved ACTA: humans conduct the interviews, AI handles transcription and structuring. The result: faster, more affordable, and through the optimal division of labor, even better.

Integrating existing knowledge: we don't start from scratch. We read in your existing documentation and merge it with the knowledge from the interviews. In doing so, we surface contradictions between document and expert statement.

02

Humans + AI

Our AI assistant analyzes interviews, identifies knowledge gaps, and generates targeted follow-up questions. Secure and GDPR-compliant.

The AI handles

  • Automatic transcription: no detail is lost
  • Automatic structuring according to the ACTA schema
  • Gap analysis between sessions: „the reasoning is still missing here"

The interviewer can focus fully on the expert. No note-taking, no thinking about structure, just full concentration on the right questions.

The result: deeper conversations, more complete documentation, and questions a human alone would have missed.

Data security: GDPR-compliant, and on request our system runs entirely on your own infrastructure.

The Process

Results in 3–7 weeks

Three clearly timed phases. Your experts invest hours, not weeks.

1
1–2 WEEKS

Scoping

Together we define scope and goal: which knowledge, for whom, in what form?

Details

What we define together

  • Which knowledge is involved, and what is its scope?
  • Who are the experts: one person or several?
  • What is the goal: onboarding, AI knowledge base, both?
  • Who is the target audience for the processed knowledge?

We also check: which documentation already exists across your different systems and can be integrated, as well as your data protection and data processing requirements.

The result: a clear project plan and an interviewer who understands your technical language.

2
1–3 WEEKS

Interviews

Structured conversations across several sessions. We capture not just the what, but the why.

Details

The three-part approach

  • Capture breadth: map all knowledge areas
  • Explore depth: dig deeper on critical topics
  • Close gaps: what happens when X is unavailable?

Each session lasts 60–120 minutes, focused but not exhausting.

After each interview: immediate consolidation and linking with the existing knowledge network. This lets us prepare the next conversation precisely.

The expert only has to answer. We lead, document, and structure.

3
1–2 WEEKS

Processing

Final structuring and delivery in formats that fit your systems.

Details

For your team

  • Onboarding guides for new employees
  • Decision trees for critical situations
  • Checklists for recurring tasks

For your systems

  • Structured knowledge base (Markdown, HTML, Word)
  • Directly integrable into your AI solution
  • Or: we deliver a ready-made expert chatbot

Together we make sure the knowledge arrives where it's needed.
